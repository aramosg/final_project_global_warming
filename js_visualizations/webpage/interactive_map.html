Welcome to our interactive map
Please, explore this map which exposes three different average metrics:
  • Average CO2 emissions per country within the analyzed period of time
  • Average temperature increase per country within the analyzed period of time
  • Average number of deaths by natural disasters per country within the analyzed period of time
Feel free to activate/deactivate the different layers of information and discover the relationship between the presented data sets. You can also change the map type to the one that better suit your preferences.
